💬 How to say your name in English
Your name Elxan is often mispronounced in English. British people may say "Elk-san" — but the correct pronunciation is closer to "El-khan" (like "khan" in Genghis Khan).

You can say: "My name is Elxan — it's pronounced El-khan." 🗣️

📘 Grammar Basics (A1 / A2)

Simple rules you'll use in everyday English and in the visa interview.

🔹 To be — I am / You are / We are

I amI am from Azerbaijan.
You areYou are kind.
He / She isShe is my wife.
We areWe are married.
They areThey are my family.
Azərbaycanca: "olmaq" feli. "I am" = "Mənəm". Bu felin formaları cins və şəxsdən asılı olaraq dəyişir.

🔹 To have — I have / We have

I haveI have one brother.
You haveYou have a nice family.
He / She hasShe has two children.
We haveWe have a small house.
Azərbaycanca: "have" = "var". "I have a brother" = "Mənim qardaşım var".

🔹 Present Simple — I live / I work / I study

I liveI live in Baku.
I workI work in a shop.
I studyI study English every day.
He / She livesShe lives in London.
Tip: With he / she / it we add -s → "He works", "She studies".

🔹 Question Forms

Where do you live?I live in Baku.
What do you do?I am a teacher.
How are you?I am fine, thank you.
What is your name?My name is Aysel.
Do you have children?Yes, I have two children.
Azərbaycanca: "Where do you live?" = "Sən harada yaşayırsan?". İngiliscədə sual sözü ("where, what, how") cümlənin əvvəlində gəlir.
